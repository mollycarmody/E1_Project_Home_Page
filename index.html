OpenSpace


Project Overview

OpenSpace will be a trusted peer-to-peer marketplace for finding cheap, accessible storage. Hosts can post to OpenSpace when they have extra space in their homes that they are willing to "rent out" to a user to store their personal items. OpenSpace will act as a passive income for hosts, while also providing users simple storage means. OpenSpace redefines the storage market by being cheap, flexible, and convenient.


Project Roadmap
